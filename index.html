6+ years of teaching experience

Through proven effort and tenacity I have developed many ways of teaching students, especially students that may be nervous on their first try.

DMV Appointments

I offer a vehicle if the student desires to take the driving test, but does not own their own car. Just make an appointment, I pick up the student and take them back.

Practice Exams

I offer practice examinations to students that want to practice before their exam. I know which mistakes you cannot afford to make and will grade you on your performance.

Bilingual

I speak both English and Spanish, my goal is to make communcation as fluid as possible. I believe that communication between the student and teacher is essential to success.
